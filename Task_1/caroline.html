Caroline Forbes

Caroline Forbes is a central character in The Vampire Diaries. Initially introduced as a high school student, she starts as an insecure, perfectionist, and sometimes self-centered girl. After being turned into a vampire, she undergoes significant growth, becoming strong, compassionate, and fiercely loyal to her friends and loved ones. Caroline is known for her bubbly personality, but she also shows resilience, especially in protecting her family and friends. Her relationships with characters like Stefan and Klaus highlight her development from a vulnerable girl to a confident, powerful vampire.
