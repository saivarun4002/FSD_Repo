Jules

Jules is a recurring character in The Vampire Diaries, known for being a tough and vengeful werewolf. She arrives in Mystic Falls seeking revenge for her friend Mason Lockwood's death. Jules is fiercely protective of her werewolf pack and often comes into conflict with the vampires, especially the Salvatore brothers. She embodies the fierce and loyal nature of werewolves, with a strong sense of duty to her kind, but her ruthless pursuit of justice sometimes puts her at odds with other characters.
