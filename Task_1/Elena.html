Elena Gilbert

Elena Gilbert, the main protagonist of The Vampire Diaries, starts as a compassionate and kind-hearted high school girl grieving the loss of her parents. Her life changes when she falls in love with the vampire brothers, Stefan and Damon Salvatore. Throughout the series, Elena faces numerous challenges, including becoming a vampire herself. Despite the darkness in her life, she remains brave, selfless, and deeply caring, constantly fighting to protect her friends and family.
